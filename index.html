About Me

Hi! I'm Brandon, a sophomore at RIT majoring in cybersecurity. I'm active in RIT's main cybersecurity club, where I participate in Physical Vulnerability, Vulnerability Research, and Incident Response groups. I also play for the men's ultimate frisbee B team where I'm treasurer and safety officer and am involved in the car club. I regularly compete in cybersecurity competitions—most recently, I placed 10th out of about 2,100 participants in the DoD Cyber Sentinel for June 2025 and won $500 USD. I write up some of my competition challenges, which you can read here.

Outside of school, I enjoy rock climbing and building watches. Climbing challenges me to think ahead and adapt under pressure, while watch building demands focus and precision with every small detail. Both give me a balance of problem-solving and creativity outside of cyber. I also like to read when I have the time.
